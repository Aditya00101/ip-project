Edisons View

The Edisons VIew by Talita Nogueira Arquitetura is a great example of this, combining different artificial lighting techniques in an artistic and minimalist environment. In the living room, for example, linear recessed luminaires on the ceiling provide wall grazing and wall washing effects that highlight the artworks. In addition, some environments have secondary lighting, such as directional pendant light fixtures over the table in the dining room
